Caipirinha

Ingredients

  • 2 lime
  • 2 tablespoons of sugar
  • 6 ice cubes
  • Vodka
  • Medium-sized glass

How to Make it

  1. Prepare the Limes:
    Cut the 2 limes into slices, place them in a glass, add the sugar, and mash the limes to release their juice.
  2. Add Ice:
    Fill the glass with ice cubes.

  3. Pour Vodka:
    Top the glass with vodka.

  4. Shake:
    Take an empty glass, cover the glass with the ingredients, and shake them together for about 1 minute.
