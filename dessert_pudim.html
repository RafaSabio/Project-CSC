Pudim

Ingredients

  • 1 can of condensed milk
  • 1 can of milk (use the condensed milk can to measure)
  • 3 whole eggs
  • 1 cup of sugar
  • 1/2 cup of water

How to Make it

  1. Blend the Ingredients:
    In a blender, mix the eggs until well combined. Add the condensed milk and milk, and blend again.

  2. Prepare the Caramel:
    In a pan, melt the sugar until it turns golden brown. Carefully add the water and let it thicken into a syrup.

  3. Prepare the Mold:
    Pour the caramel sauce into a round mold and spread it evenly. Pour the pudding mixture on top of the caramel.

  4. Bake in a Water Bath:
    Place the mold inside a larger baking dish filled with water. Bake at medium heat for 45 minutes.

  5. Check Doneness:
    Insert a toothpick into the pudding; if it comes out clean, it is done.

  6. Cool and Unmold:
    Allow the pudding to cool completely before inverting it onto a serving plate.
