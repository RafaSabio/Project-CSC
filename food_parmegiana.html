Parmegiana

Ingredients

    • 1kg filet mignon, cut into steaks
    • 2 eggs
    • Salt, to taste
    • Minced garlic, to taste
    • 1 tablespoon butter (for frying)
    • 300g mozzarella cheese, sliced
    • Oregano, to taste
    • All-purpose flour, for breading
    • Breadcrumbs, for breading
    • Black pepper, to taste
    • Olive oil, for seasoning
    • Tomato sauce, to taste
    • Grated cheese, to taste
    • Olives, for garnish

How to Make it

  1. Prepare the Steaks:
    Slice the filet mignon into thin steaks. Season with salt, black pepper, minced garlic, and a drizzle of olive oil. Let it marinate for 20 minutes.

  2. Bread the Steaks:
    Dredge the steaks first in all-purpose flour, then dip into beaten eggs (lightly seasoned with salt), and finally coat with breadcrumbs. Shake off excess at each step.

  3. Fry the Steaks:
    Heat butter and a little olive oil in a frying pan. Fry the breaded steaks on both sides until golden brown. Place them on paper towels to drain excess oil.

  4. Prepare the Sauce:
    Make a simple tomato sauce using your favorite recipe or a store-bought one. Season to taste.

  5. Assemble the Dish:
    Spread a layer of tomato sauce in a baking dish. Lay the fried steaks over the sauce.

  6. Add the Toppings:
    Cover the steaks with more tomato sauce, then layer generously with mozzarella slices. Sprinkle with grated cheese and oregano.

  7. Bake:
    Preheat your oven to 180°C (350°F). Bake the dish for approximately 20 minutes, or until the cheese melts and bubbles.

  8. Garnish and Serve:
    Garnish with olives. Serve with white rice and French fries for a complete meal.
